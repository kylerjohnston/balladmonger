What is Balladmonger?

Balladmonger is a small web app built with Python and Flask which dynamically generates poems—or “ballads”—based on Shakespeare’s King Lear and other related works. I wrote Balladmonger in the fall of 2015 as a project for a graduate seminar at the University of Illinois on the interactions between Shakespearian drama and early modern ballads. Its purpose is to remediate King Lear into a ballad (well, multiple ballads) and a digital media object, interrogating in the process our ideas of what a work of literature is (can we call what Balladmonger produces remediations of King Lear?) and destabilizing (I hope) ideas of “authority” and “authorship.”
